# Welcome, to my hardware journey!🚴
Arduino has been the most fun thing to learn so far along and the realms that hardware open up is truly amazing✨

## Why?
Working in Accessibility field, especially in India, teaches you the importance of creating low cost solutions which are tangible and practical to use. I had been working on the AI and Software side realising that less than 0.5% of the Persons with Visual Impairment (PVI) in our country have the access to such systems and can make the effective use out of it. While the complete team of NCAHT is on the mission to empower as many PVI as they can, the short term goal is to create handy and everyday life solutions which can be utilised by them
